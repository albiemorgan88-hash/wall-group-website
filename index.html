Building Communities Since 2008

The Wall Group began with a single phone call in October 2008 about a small filling station on Crumlin Road. What started as a 468 square foot operation has grown into Northern Ireland's most dynamic convenience retail network.

Founded by Gavan Wall, a former criminal barrister who left his legal career to pursue his passion for business, The Wall Group now operates 12+ stores across Belfast and Northern Ireland, employing over 250 staff members.

From SPAR convenience stores to Subway restaurants, from innovative concepts like YoggieBerrie to essential filling stations, we're committed to being at the heart of every community we serve.

"We're unique in that we're trying to build a business of scale in Belfast. We're not afraid of failure. We will achieve it." — Gavan Wall, Founder
